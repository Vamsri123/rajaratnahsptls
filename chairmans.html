Chairmans Message

  • Home
  • Chairmans Message

RRHET is Committed to providing access to Medical and Educational services,to the masses in remote areas where the need is acutely felt.

Rajaratna Healthcare and Educational Trust( RRHET ), a non-profit organization, has ambitious plans to make Healthcare and Education accessible to the poor people with a noble aim to reach the masses in remote villages. Because we believe that Navyandhra dreams are possible to realise only when villages are uplifted.

RRHET is Committed to providing access to Medical and Educational services,to the masses in remote areas where the need is acutely felt. The Trust's motto is "Health is the First Wealth", and the people in rural areas will spend their lifetime earnings to get medical care, unless it is made available to them within their means. We believe that Govt alone cannot fight such cause fully, but Private Sector must equally join hands. This PPP mode will help to achieve the goals. This will also ensure people's participations in the development process which is sustainable in the long run. RRHET is committed this philosophy. This philosophy guides our every action.

To afford corporate medical treatment to the poor, Rajaratna Health Care and Educational Trust, has started its programs here at Ramachandra Puram AP, with Thousand Fifty Bedded Hospital, to lift the rural people out of this situation where they are getting medical treatment with money saved for entire life but with debt to pay for life. We are committed to undo this trend by making a humble beginning in a silent revolution nucleated at RCPM Raja Ratna Nagar. Future will herald more such centres.

Here we are determined to make medicine accessible to the masses with state-of-the-art operation theaters with most modern equipment and facilities that are not available in the surrounding areas and for which you all support and bless us. We are sure when we join hands we can create miracles in this forgotten area but potential people with good hearts and rich culture.

Our commitment is already visible in the infrastructure and development we have so far brought. More is coming in future.
